Our Products

Premium plastic sheets for every application

Polycarbonate Sheets

Our polycarbonate sheets are known for their exceptional impact resistance, optical clarity, and thermal stability. Perfect for applications requiring durability and transparency.

Clear Polycarbonate Sheets

Features: Crystal clear transparency, UV protection, impact resistant up to 250 times stronger than glass.

Applications: Skylights, greenhouse panels, protective barriers, machine guards.

Available Thicknesses: 2mm, 3mm, 4mm, 5mm, 6mm, 8mm, 10mm

Colored Polycarbonate Sheets

Features: Available in bronze, gray, blue, and green tints while maintaining excellent impact resistance.

Applications: Architectural glazing, privacy screens, decorative panels.

Available Thicknesses: 2mm, 3mm, 4mm, 5mm, 6mm, 8mm

Multiwall Polycarbonate

Features: Lightweight, excellent thermal insulation, easy to install with built-in channels.

Applications: Greenhouses, conservatories, canopies, roofing systems.

Available Thicknesses: 4mm, 6mm, 8mm, 10mm, 16mm, 25mm

Acrylic Sheets

High-quality acrylic sheets offering excellent optical clarity and weather resistance. A versatile material for indoor and outdoor applications.

Clear Acrylic Sheets

Features: Excellent optical clarity, half the weight of glass, easy to fabricate and machine.

Applications: Displays, signage, aquariums, picture frames, protective screens.

Available Thicknesses: 1mm, 2mm, 3mm, 4mm, 5mm, 6mm, 8mm, 10mm, 12mm

Colored Acrylic Sheets

Features: Available in a wide range of colors and finishes including matte, gloss, and fluorescent.

Applications: Artistic displays, architectural features, retail fixtures, lighting panels.

Available Colors: Red, blue, green, yellow, black, white, and many more

Frosted Acrylic Sheets

Features: Translucent finish provides privacy while allowing light transmission.

Applications: Privacy screens, light diffusers, bathroom partitions, office dividers.

Available Thicknesses: 2mm, 3mm, 4mm, 5mm, 6mm, 8mm

Specialty Plastic Sheets

We also carry a range of specialty plastic materials for specific applications and industries.

PETG Sheets

Features: Excellent chemical resistance, FDA approved for food contact, easy to thermoform.

Applications: Food service equipment, medical devices, retail displays.

PVC Sheets

Features: Cost-effective, good chemical resistance, available in various colors and textures.

Applications: Industrial applications, signage, construction panels.

Polyethylene Sheets

Features: Excellent chemical resistance, low friction surface, FDA approved options available.

Applications: Cutting boards, marine applications, chemical tanks.
